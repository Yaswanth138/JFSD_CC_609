Good Food: A Guide to Healthy Eating

Fruits & Vegetables

Eating a variety of fruits and vegetables is essential for providing essential vitamins and minerals to your body. They are rich in fiber and antioxidants, helping to improve digestion and protect your cells from damage.

Whole Grains

Whole grains such as oats, brown rice, and quinoa are full of nutrients, fiber, and protein. They help regulate your blood sugar and provide long-lasting energy throughout the day.

Lean Proteins

Lean proteins, including chicken, fish, and plant-based proteins like tofu and legumes, are vital for muscle repair and overall growth. They are low in unhealthy fats, making them a great choice for a balanced diet.
